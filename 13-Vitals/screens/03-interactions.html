Ibuprofen (Advil)

NSAID · Pain relief / Anti-inflammatory

warning2 Interactions Found
SERIOUS

Ibuprofen + Aspirin

Concurrent use may reduce the cardioprotective effect of aspirin. Ibuprofen can block aspirin's ability to thin the blood.

Recommendation: Take aspirin at least 30 minutes before ibuprofen, or use acetaminophen instead.

MODERATE

Ibuprofen + Lisinopril

NSAIDs may reduce the blood pressure lowering effect of ACE inhibitors. May also increase risk of kidney problems.

Recommendation: Monitor blood pressure closely. Consider acetaminophen as an alternative.
