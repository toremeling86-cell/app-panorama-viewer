The Challenge

Luxe, a luxury e-commerce app, struggled with a 1.2% conversion rate and high cart abandonment. Users found the checkout flow confusing and the browse experience uninspiring.

Key Takeaway

"The biggest win wasn't visual — it was reducing checkout from 5 steps to 2. Simplicity drove conversion more than aesthetics."
